\begin{abstractzh}
在社会前行与时代加速变迁的大背景下，对销售人员进行培训已成为现代企业中的关键环节。这不仅能够增强员工的专业技能和个人素养，还能够推动企业的全面发展。鉴于日益激烈的竞争环境和不断进步的技术，企业必须持续更新，以保证员工掌握必需的技巧和信息。

本文将分为六个部分进行探讨，首先为引言；其次，将介绍相关的理论基础，包括成人学习理论、期望理论和柯克帕特里克的四级培训评估模型；接下来，将具体分析天津通和饲料有限公司当前的销售人员培训情况；随后，将剖析该公司在销售人员培训中面临的问题；第五部分，将提出解决这些问题的策略；最后，为全文的结论。

\keywordszh{销售人员培训；培训需求；培训效果评估}
\end{abstractzh}

\begin{abstracten}
In the background of social progress and the accelerating change of The Times, the training of sales personnel has become a key link in modern enterprises. This can not only enhance the professional skills and personal quality of employees, but also promote the overall development of the enterprise.

This paper will be divided into six parts, first, the introduction; second, the relevant theoretical basis, including adult learning theory, expectation theory and Kirkpatrick's four-level training evaluation model; the analysis of the current situation of sales personnel training in Tianjin Tonghe Feed Co., Ltd.; then, the analysis of the problems faced by the company in sales personnel training; the fifth part, the strategies to solve these problems; finally, the conclusion of the full text.

\keywordsen{sales personnel training; training needs; training effect evaluation}
\end{abstracten}
